    About Us At Bookies.Org

    Bookies.Org is an intiative interested in putting book lovers, authors and vendors in one space where we aim to connect readers and writers to one central unit of book loving togetherness. Bookies once a bookshop opened in 2008 at Victoria Island. Later this bookshop grew to many more quaters around Lagos Nigeria. The decision to kickstart its digital parent hub came in 2012 and since then we have grown to house about 3 million writers from all across the globe, 47 million hard and softcopies available to all members and train about 200 schools on more reasons to read.

    Our Team

    Our Team is made up of professionals from all over the world. From our family at the office pulling off the average 9-5 work time range to our online guest writers/contributors to the various men and women working at the backend where we make sure your transactions with us are made smooth and without hitches, bookies.org is made up of everyone, both me and you.
      But even teams have to have people who step in to take charge of things, to sort of hold the tide when it comes, the people who stand between the good and the bad, those who take the fall for us all. From Founders to managing directors,interns and technical staff everyone matters.
